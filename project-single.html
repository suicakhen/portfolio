Comfort Shoes

UI/UX Design

The Comfort Shoes app is an online store selling a collection of comfortable shoes for both kids and adults. It offers a seamless shopping experience with a focus on styles that prioritize comfort.
This project involves designing the UI/UX for Comfort Shoes mobile app. I used Concepts software for the initial sketching. As I progressed through the design journey, I transitioned to Figma, utilizing its robust tools to refine and complete the remaining aspects of the design process.

Project goal

The goals of this project are - make a sale to customer, order tracting, increase sales, mobile accesibility, enhance user experiances.

Idenifying End users

The end users of this project are women and men all ages, children over 12, athletics, mobile users, shopper.

Stakeholders

Business owner, desinger, financial institution, developer, customer,investors.
